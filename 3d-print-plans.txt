Bambu A1 Tool Plans
====================

All tools designed for Bambu A1 build volume (256 x 256 x 256mm).
Use Bambu Studio with 0.4mm nozzle.


Axle Bender Jig
----------------
Precision jig for bending BSA axles to exact angles. Features a protractor
scale and pin vise slot. Print in PLA at 0.16mm layer height for accuracy.

  Size:     40 x 25 x 15mm
  Infill:   80%
  Time:     ~25 min
  Material: PLA


Wheel Alignment Gauge
----------------------
Slides over the car body to check wheel alignment and camber. Two rails
reference the body sides while indicator arms touch the wheel face.

  Size:     80 x 75 x 20mm
  Infill:   40%
  Time:     ~45 min
  Material: PLA


Weight Placement Template
--------------------------
Custom template matching your car body profile. Drop-in pockets for tungsten
cubes positioned at the calculated optimal CoG location.

  Size:     Custom to car
  Infill:   20%
  Time:     ~35 min
  Material: PLA


Axle Slot Guide
----------------
Clamp-on drill guide that ensures axle slots are perfectly straight and at
the correct height. Fits over the stock BSA block.

  Size:     50 x 45 x 30mm
  Infill:   60%
  Time:     ~40 min
  Material: PLA
